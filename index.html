UX Designer

My Projects

CryoKliniek

CryoKliniek was my first project. I created a website that offers different Cryolipolysis packages to clients in order to help them lose fat. Clients can read a lot of information about the treatments on the website and afterwards book an appoaintment.

StichtingNee

One of my biggest projects was making the website for StichtingNee. The foundation needed a website with a lot of information about drugs and alcohol. Besides making the websites user friendly I also needed to do research in order to write the information sections on the website. Currently I am still working for the foundation in order to update information and maintain the website.

Giatelli

Giatelli is he biggest project I have worked on. The company delivers high quality italian eyewear with prescription glasses. I am involved in the layout and web desgin of the website. Currently the websites is under construction. We are currently working on the front- and back end of the website. The website will be ready to go online at the end of 2021.

Stichting DPJ

The website that I created for this foundation was also a very big project. I needed to do research in order to write the articles about drugs and the effects they have on people. After making different wireframes using Adobe XD I started developing the website. I made sure everything was clean and simple to understand for users. I combined my style in the process by giving the website a elegant and simplistic design. Currently I still do the maintenance of the website.

Covid-19 Kliniek

As the pandemic started in 2020 I was approached by a company in order to make a website where customers can book a appoinment for COVID-19 test. People are able to book a PCR test or Covid Rapid Test on the website. The website also offers a lot of information about the tests so people are able to read which products are used for the tests. Currently I am still one the people that maintains and updates the website. Besides that I also provide advice regarding the web design and UI.
